AI Videos - What are they?

AI videos are videos created with generative artificial inteligence using a prompt and some context.

How can AI videos be good?

  1. Ai videos can be used for learning purposes
  2. Ai videos can be used to save time during certain projects if done the right way.

How can AI videos be bad?

  1. They can be used to make deepfakes for scams
  2. It takes from the creativity of apps like Youtube or Tiktok
